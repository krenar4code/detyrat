The Easiest Gluten-Free Bread Recipe on the Internet

Published on Dec 21, 2022

Did someone say "fresh-baked gluten-free bread"? When it comes to gluten-free recipes, few options work as well as our bread mix. From delicious cookies to decadent brownies and flavorful pizzas, we’re dedicated to making all your favorites without compromising taste.

Besides more Partake Classic Baking Mix, this gluten-free bread recipe is one of the easiest ways to bake a loaf that’s soft and chewy inside, with a golden crust outside. Plus, it’s free of the top 9 allergens.

Gluten-Free Bread

Here’s what you’ll need:

Ingredients

  • 1 Packet of yeast
  • 1 ½ cups warm water (110°F - 115°F)
  • 1 ½ tsp apple cider vinegar
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 box of Partake Bread Mix

Instructions

  1. Combine yeast, warm water, sugar, and apple cider vinegar in a bowl. Let sit for 5 minutes until frothy.
  2. In a separate bowl, mix Partake Bread Mix with salt. Slowly add the yeast mixture and olive oil to the dry ingredients, stirring to combine.
  3. Knead the dough for 5 minutes, then shape it into a loaf.
  4. Place the loaf in a greased bread pan, cover, and let it rise for 1 hour.
  5. Bake at 375°F for 30-35 minutes until golden brown.
  6. Allow to cool completely before slicing.

Note: Bread is easier to handle and slice once it has sat for at least 24 hours or a minimum of overnight.
